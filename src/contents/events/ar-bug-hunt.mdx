The **AR Bug Hunt** is an exciting event that takes place around the college, guided by hints. Participants will explore the campus, uncovering hidden bugs through augmented reality for a thrilling and interactive experience!

### What to Expect
- **Augmented Reality Fun**: Use your devices to find and interact with hidden AR bugs scattered throughout the campus.
- **Exploration**: Discover new areas of the college while following clues and hints to locate the bugs.
- **Prizes**: Compete for rewards based on the number of bugs found and the creativity shown during the hunt.

In every techfest, there are at least **75+ participants**, making this a fun and engaging experience filled with adventure and excitement!
