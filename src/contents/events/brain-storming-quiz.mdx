The **Brain Storming Quiz** is a week-long event designed to challenge participants to brainstorm creative solutions to engaging questions related to tech and engineering. This interactive competition encourages critical thinking and innovation while fostering collaboration among participants.

### What to Expect
- **Challenging Questions**: Tackle a variety of thought-provoking questions that stimulate creativity and problem-solving skills.
- **Collaboration**: Work together with fellow participants to brainstorm and develop innovative solutions.
- **Prizes**: Compete for exciting awards and recognition for the most creative ideas.

In every techfest, there are at least **100+ participants**, making this a fun and engaging experience filled with inspiration and ingenuity!
