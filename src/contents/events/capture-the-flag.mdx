**Capture the Flag** is a cybersecurity challenge where participants search for hidden flags to test their hacking skills. This competitive event encourages players to employ various techniques and strategies to uncover vulnerabilities and demonstrate their cybersecurity expertise.

### What to Expect
- **Skill Testing**: Engage in challenges that push your hacking skills to the limit as you search for flags.
- **Hands-On Learning**: Learn new techniques and strategies in a practical, competitive environment.
- **Recognition**: Showcase your cybersecurity prowess and compete for awards based on your performance.

In every techfest, there are at least **75+ participants**, making this a fun and engaging experience filled with learning and friendly competition!
