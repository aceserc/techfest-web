The **Datathon** is a data-driven competition where participants analyze large datasets and train machine learning models to solve real-world problems. This event challenges teams to develop, optimize, and present their models, showcasing their data science and machine learning expertise.

### What to Expect
- **Hands-On Experience**: Work with real datasets to gain practical experience in data analysis and machine learning.
- **Team Collaboration**: Collaborate with your team to brainstorm ideas, develop solutions, and refine your models.
- **Presentation Opportunity**: Showcase your work to judges and receive feedback on your innovative approaches.

In every techfest, there are at least **100+ participants**, making this a fun and engaging experience filled with insight and innovation!
