The **Hackathon** is an intense event where teams collaborate to develop innovative software solutions within a limited time frame. Participants will brainstorm, design, and build functional prototypes, focusing on creativity, technical expertise, and practical problem-solving.

### What to Expect
- **Collaborative Development**: Work in teams to tackle real-world challenges and bring your ideas to life.
- **Mentorship**: Gain insights and guidance from industry experts throughout the event.
- **Demo Day**: Present your projects to judges for a chance to win exciting prizes and recognition.

In every techfest, there are at least **75+ participants**, making this a fun and engaging experience filled with creativity and innovation!
