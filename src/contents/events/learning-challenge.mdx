The **Learning Challenge** is an engaging event where participants tackle new skills and knowledge through a series of interactive tasks and activities. This challenge promotes hands-on learning and encourages participants to step outside their comfort zones.

### What to Expect
- **Skill Development**: Engage in tasks designed to help you acquire new skills in a fun and interactive way.
- **Collaborative Activities**: Work alongside fellow participants to tackle challenges and share knowledge.
- **Prizes**: Compete for exciting awards and recognition for your achievements throughout the challenge.

In every techfest, there are at least **150+ participants**, making this a fun and engaging experience filled with inspiration and personal growth!
