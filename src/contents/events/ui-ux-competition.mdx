The **UI/UX Competition** is a creative showdown where designers craft intuitive and engaging digital experiences. This competition challenges participants to innovate and design seamless user interfaces that enhance user interaction and satisfaction.

### What to Expect
- **Creative Design**: Showcase your ability to create visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces.
- **Feedback from Experts**: Receive insights and critiques from industry professionals to refine your designs.
- **Prizes**: Compete for exciting awards and recognition for your innovative design solutions.

In every techfest, there are at least **75+ participants**, making this a fun and engaging experience filled with creativity and collaboration!
