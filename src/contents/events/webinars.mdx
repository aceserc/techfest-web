The **Webinar** is an online session featuring expert discussions and insights on a specific topic. This event aims to enlighten participants with valuable knowledge from industry leaders, fostering a dynamic learning environment.


### What to Expect
- **Expert Insights**: Gain knowledge from industry leaders and thought-provoking speakers.
- **Interactive Q&A**: Participate in engaging sessions where you can ask questions and deepen your understanding.
- **Networking Opportunities**: Connect with fellow participants and expand your professional network.

In every techfest, there are at least **200+ participants**, making this a fun and engaging experience filled with learning and collaboration!
